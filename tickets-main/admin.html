Admin - Upload 2026 Moments Photos

📋 Instructions

Follow these steps to upload photos for the 2026 Moments gallery:

  • Click the blue button below to select photo files from your computer
  • You can select multiple photos at once
  • Preview the selected photos below
  • Click "Upload Photos" to upload them
  • Photos will be saved to the images/uploads/ folder
