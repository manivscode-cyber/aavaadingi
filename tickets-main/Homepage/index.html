About Aavaadingi

About Aavaa Dingi

Aavaa Dingi is an international inclusive music and dance festival held in Chennai, India, dedicated to empowering underprivileged youth and individuals with disabilities.

Organized by DCF Voice, the festival provides a stage where local and international artists perform alongside differently-abled performers, celebrating inclusivity through fine arts and cultural expression.

Our Vision

To celebrate inclusivity through fine arts and cultural performances while supporting the sustainable livelihood of challenged individuals.

About Aavaadingi in Pictures

Our Mission

To create an inclusive platform where differently-abled performers and underprivileged youth can showcase their talents alongside international artists, celebrating diversity through music, dance, and cultural expression.

Our Vision

To establish Aavaadingi as a beacon of inclusivity in the cultural world, supporting sustainable livelihoods for challenged individuals while promoting fine arts and empowering communities across Chennai and beyond.
